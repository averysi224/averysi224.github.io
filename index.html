About Me

I am a fifth-year PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on developing trustworthy machine learning systems through uncertainty quantification and robust optimization, primarily from a statistical, distribution-free perspective. Previously, I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Automation from the Institute of Information Science at Tsinghua University in 2018, and my Master of Science in Computer Vision from the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University in 2019.

Research Interests

  • Distribution-free uncertainty quantification
  • Robust optimization
  • Trustworthy machine learning for healthcare

I'm open to collaborations or talking about anything related to ML and research -- I especially enjoy mentoring younger students interested in getting involved in the field! Feel free to reach out at wenwens@seas.upenn.edu.
